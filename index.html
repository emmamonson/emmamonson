Puppy Chow

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 15
Ingredients Quantity
Peanut Butter 1 cup
Chocolate Chips 1 cup
Chex Cereal 1 box
Powdered Sugar 1 bag

Step 1: Melt the peanut butter and chocolate chips in a pot, mixing them together-making sure they do not burn.

Step 2: Pour the cereal into a large bowl. Now pour the melted mix over the cereal.

Step 3: Mix the melted chocolate and peanut butter with the cereal until it is completely covered.

Step 4: Pour powdered sugar over the cereal (start with about a cup and add more if needed) until all the cereal is covered.

Step 5: Enjoy!

Source: JoyFoodSunshine-Puppy Chow

Cheese Quesadilla

  • Time: 15 minutes
  • Serves: 1-2
Ingredients Quantity
Torillas 2
Shredded Cheese 1 cup
Salsa Optional
Sour Cream Optional

Step 1: Put the first tortilla in the bottom of the pan.

Step 2: Pour the cheese on the tortilla, and put the second tortilla on top of it.

Step 3: Check on the cheese and wait until it melts, then flip it over so both sides get golden brown.

Step 4: Take it out of the pan and use a pizza cutter to cut it into triangles.

Step 5: Eat it plain or with salsa and sour cream. Enjoy!

Source: Campbell'sKitchen-Quesadilla

Smoothie

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredients Quantity
Strawberries 2 cups
Bananas 1
Greek Yogurt 1/2 cup
Milk 1/2 cup

Step 1: Blend all ingredients in the blender.

Step 2: Get it to the thickness that you want and enjoy!
